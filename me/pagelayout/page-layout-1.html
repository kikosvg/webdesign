Introduction to Quantum Physics

Quantum physics has been a profound source of motivation for me ever since I first encountered it in high school. The initial exposure to its principles and mysteries captivated my imagination, sparking a deep curiosity about the fundamental workings of the universe that classical physics could not satisfy.

My Story

Unlike classical physics, which follows the predictable paths laid out by Newton's laws, quantum physics presented an entirely different, almost mysterious realm. Particles could defy common logic, existing in two places simultaneously or even in states that seemed contradictory. Even more astonishing was the phenomenon of entanglement, where objects could remain interconnected and influence each other instantaneously across vast distances, defying the conventional limits of space and time. This strange, counterintuitive world suggested that reality was far more complex and interconnected than I had previously imagined."
